VNPS Photography Programs

VNPS offers three photography courses: basic, intermediate, and advance photography. Its program lasts 9 months for a total of 36 three hour sessions. The courses start in February, few weeks after the Vietnamese Tet (the Lunar New Year) and end in Novemver, class session occurs on Sundays at the Willson Center, 6131 Willston Drive, Falls Church, VA 22042.

Because each students has different backgrounds and experiences and are from all walks of life, each year the program has been modified to suit individual needs but also fulfill the VNPS requirements for each level. The below are approved programs for 2016.

To request information on the program and how to register for VNPS photography course, please send email to info@dcvnps.org
